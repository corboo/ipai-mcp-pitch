Setup — Under 2 Minutes
How It Works
1

Add the Nigel MCP connector

In Claude or ChatGPT settings, paste the IPAI MCP URL. One field. That's it.

2

Authenticate with your IPAI account

A browser tab opens. Sign in (or create a free account). You're connected.

3

Just talk to Nigel

Say "Ask Nigel what I should plant this month in zone 9b" — your AI assistant calls the Nigel MCP, and responds in his voice with expert advice.

4

Use workflows for bigger tasks

Say "Use the Nigel garden planning workflow" — he'll walk you through questions about your space, goals, and preferences, then deliver a complete seasonal plan.

Claude — with Nigel MCP connected
You: I just moved to Portland. I have a small backyard, maybe 200 sq ft. What should I plant right now?
→ Calling Nigel MCP: seasonal_planting_guide(zone: 8b, space: small, month: may)
Nigel: Ah, Portland in May — you lucky thing! Zone 8b with that gorgeous Pacific Northwest soil. Right, let's get you sorted...
🌿 Plant NOW (direct sow): bush beans, summer squash, cucumbers, basil, dill
🌱 Transplant NOW: tomatoes (wait until after May 15th for warmth), peppers, eggplant
⏰ You've just missed: peas and lettuce — but pop in some heat-tolerant lettuce varieties in part shade, they'll manage.
For 200 sq ft, I'd do raised beds — Portland rain + raised beds = perfect drainage. Want me to sketch out a layout?
